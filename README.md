# 🚀 VaultPay — Trustless International Payments

## 🌍 The Problem
As international students, we constantly deal with high fees, taxes, and uncertainty when making large international payments. On top of that, sending money to someone you don’t know introduces serious trust issues.

> Can you really trust the person on the other side?

---

## 💡 Our Solution
**VaultPay** is a stablecoin-powered escrow platform designed to make international payments:

- 🔒 Secure — Funds are locked until both parties confirm
- 💸 Low-cost — Avoid traditional banking fees and taxes
- 🤝 Trust-driven — Built-in rating system + fraud detection

---

## ⚙️ How It Works
1. Buyer sends funds (stablecoin) into escrow  
2. Funds are securely held on-chain  
3. Once both parties confirm:
   - ✅ Funds are released to the seller  
   - ❌ Or refunded if terms are not met  
4. After completion:
   - Users rate each other  
   - A trust score and fraud signal are generated
